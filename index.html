Which artist painted "Christina's World"?

That's correct!
Andrew Wyeth painted “Christina’s World” in 1948. The woman in the painting is Anna Christina Olsen who suffered from polio and was paralyzed from the waist down. One day Wyeth saw her crawling across the field and was inspired to do the painting.
